# ADR-050: Slos And Error Budgets For Ai Platform

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Operational excellence requires reproducible deployment, controlled change, and measurable service health.

## Decision
The platform standardizes on slos and error budgets for ai platform as part of the reference architecture.

## Consequences
This decision improves reliability, traceability, and supportability across platform releases and day-2 operations.
