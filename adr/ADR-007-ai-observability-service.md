# ADR-007 AI Observability Service

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The reference architecture requires a clear design decision for ai observability service.

## Decision
Telemetry, audit correlation, lineage, and cost tracking are first-class platform services.

## Consequences
- The architecture baseline becomes explicit and reviewable.
- The logical model remains stable across implementations.
- Downstream documents can reference the ADR as a source of truth.
