# 05 Control Plane Services

## AI Orchestrator
Plans, sequences, and supervises work. It decomposes user requests into tasks and selects business capabilities.

## AI Capability Registry
Catalog of capabilities, implementations, ownership, versions, trust level, supported tools, and deployment metadata.

## AI Runtime Service
Owns runtime state such as sessions, tasks, checkpoints, approval status, and replay information.

## AI Security Service
Performs identity federation, delegated authorization, token exchange, policy evaluation, and approval enforcement.

## AI Metadata Service
Manages prompts, tool metadata, capability metadata, model metadata, and semantic descriptors.

## AI Observability Service
Correlates traces, logs, metrics, audit, lineage, and cost into platform-level evidence.

## Audit Service
Stores immutable audit events required for compliance and forensic analysis.
