# Governance Principles

1. Treat capabilities as governed enterprise assets.
2. Separate policy definition from policy enforcement.
3. Require approval for risky or sensitive operations.
4. Govern prompts, models, tools, and connectors as versioned artifacts.
5. Use lifecycle states such as draft, approved, deprecated, and retired.
6. Make governance decisions visible in audit and observability.
7. Keep governance cross-cutting rather than embedded in a single agent.
