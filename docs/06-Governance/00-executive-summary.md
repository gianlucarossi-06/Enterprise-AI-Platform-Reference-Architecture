# Executive Summary

The governance architecture ensures that AI capabilities, prompts, models, tools, and policies are controlled as managed enterprise assets rather than as ad hoc runtime artifacts.

Governance is cross-cutting. It spans the control plane, execution plane, observability plane, and OCI implementation. The goal is to make lifecycle, approval, and risk decisions explicit, reviewable, and auditable.

## Outcomes

- controlled capability lifecycle
- model and prompt governance
- policy versioning and approvals
- consistent risk management
- auditable governance decisions
