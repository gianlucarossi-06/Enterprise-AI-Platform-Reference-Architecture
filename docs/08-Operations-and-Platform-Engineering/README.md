## Executive summary

The operations and platform engineering volume defines how the Enterprise AI Platform is built, released, operated, recovered, and improved over time. It turns the reference architecture into a repeatable delivery and support model.

## Scope

This volume covers day-2 operations, GitOps, CI/CD, resilience, backup and recovery, operational dashboards, service levels, and FinOps practices.

## Design intent

Operational excellence is treated as a platform capability, not an afterthought. The reference architecture favors reproducible automation, measurable service levels, and clear operational ownership.
